# BlockMate – Your Ultimate Ad-Free Browsing Companion 🚀

**BlockMate** is a lightweight, powerful browser extension designed to give you **full control over your online experience**. Say goodbye to intrusive ads, distracting videos, and promoted content on YouTube and across the web. Enjoy a cleaner, faster, and distraction-free browsing environment—**completely free**.  

---

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Block YouTube Shorts**  
  Remove unwanted short videos from your feed and focus on what matters.

- **Remove Promoted Videos**  
  Say goodbye to sponsored content cluttering your YouTube homepage.

- **Stealth Mode (Premium)**  
  Browse privately without leaving traces of your video history or preferences.

- **Block Ads on All Websites**  
  Enjoy an ad-free internet experience, including pop-ups, banners, and video ads.

- **Automatic Updates**  
  BlockMate continuously improves to keep up with new ads and promotions.

- **User-Friendly Settings**  
  Easily enable or disable features from a clean, intuitive interface.

- **Fully Free Option**  
  All core features are available at no cost. Premium options enhance privacy and convenience.
