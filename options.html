Our Mission

BlockMate was created to give you control over your browsing experience. Ads, popups, and distractions shouldn’t take away your focus. We believe in a cleaner internet for everyone—completely free.

Maintaining and improving this extension takes time and resources. Your support helps us keep BlockMate free, updated, and accessible to all.
